Energy for a Better World

Imagine a world where roads don't need constant maintenance and power your car with clean solar energy ☀️🚗

The Big Idea

Today’s roadways are aging fast and struggle to keep up with modern transportation demands. 🛣️ What if we built roads from ultra-durable solar materials that don't just last longer—but also power vehicles directly through contact with their tires?

Using embedded weight sensors, energy would only be transferred when a vehicle is present. It’s time we reimagine transport—cleaner, faster, and friendlier. ⚡

Materials Matter: To make this idea a reality, the road surface needs to be nearly indestructible and transparent enough to allow sunlight through. Materials like tempered laminated glass or transparent concrete could be used as top layers, providing both strength and solar transmittance. 🔍

The Physics Behind It 🔬

Photovoltaic Effect: Solar panels absorb sunlight and convert it to electricity via the photovoltaic effect using semiconductor materials (e.g., silicon). ☀️➡️⚡

Inductive Transfer: Electric energy can be transferred wirelessly through inductive coupling using two coils: one in the road, one in the car. When AC flows through the road coil, a changing magnetic field is created, inducing voltage in the car coil by Faraday’s Law: 

EMF = −N × dΦ/dt

This is like wireless phone charging, but for moving vehicles! 🌀🔋

Efficiency: With proper coil alignment and high-frequency AC, transfer efficiencies of 80–90% are achievable. 🔋➡️🚗

Frictional Motion & Energy: P = F × v. For 150 N and 20 m/s:

P = 150 N × 20 m/s = 3000 W (3 kW per vehicle at peak)

Daily Output: 100m × 3m × 8kWh = 2400 kWh/day ☀️⚙️

Problems & Solutions 🧠

Winter Weather (Canada): Solar roads can use stored energy to heat surfaces in winter—melting snow and ice! ❄️🔥

Water Management: Built-in drainage systems collect meltwater and purify it before reuse or safe release. 💧

Durability: Roads will face heavy traffic and weather. We use tough glass with anti-scratch coating and modular design for easy replacement. 🔩
